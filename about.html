About Our Project

The Carbon Exhaust Project was developed by a team of students passionate about innovation and sustainability. Our mission is to reduce industrial pollution by capturing exhaust gases and refining them into usable carbon materials.

Our Objectives

  • Collect exhaust emissions from factories and power plants.
  • Convert carbon particles into valuable raw materials.
  • Promote cleaner, more sustainable industries.

Applications

Our extracted carbon can be used in:

  • Manufacturing (inks, paints, carbon fiber)
  • Energy storage (batteries, fuel cells)
  • Construction materials
  • Filtration and purification systems
